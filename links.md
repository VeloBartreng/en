Here we want to share with use some useful links and information to make your cycling life a bit easier!

#### Veloh

You **don't have a bike or want to hop on one spontaneously?** then [Veloh](https://myveloh.lu/) is perfect for you. 

You can buy a yearly subscription for **18 Euros/Year**. Then every borrow you can ride **30 minutes for free** (more than enough time to ride from Bertrange to the City Centre). And you can and borrow bikes as often as you want.

Here in the Screenshot you get an overview where there are Veloh-stations exist.

![Veloh Map]({% link images/Screenshot from 2026-05-13 18-30-50.png %}) 

**Bonus**: Our commune is even refunding you the yearly subscription. More info [here](https://www.bertrange.lu/fr/demarches-administratives/subsides-allocations). 

#### Bikebox

You are **multimodal**? Then you possibly need to lock your bike at a trainsation. At the **bikeboxes** you can **secure you bike safely** in a secured box. The service is free to use, you only have to get access to it via this [link](https://www.cfl.lu/en-gb/withoutbarrier/detail/bikebox).
